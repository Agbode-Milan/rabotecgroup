RABOTEC SUSTAINABILITY

Our Sustainable Philosophy

At RABOTEC, we are committed to a sustainable philosophy that ensures we have a healthy and productive workforce, a robust supplier base, and a strong customer portfolio, in addition to environmentally-conscious practices.

At RABOTEC, we recognize the vital importance of adopting a sustainable philosophy to improve our way of life, as well as our industry practices. We have implemented a sustainability Strategy, which embodies our ambition to go further to reduce our environmental footprint and to have a positive, sustainable impact in all of our projects. Not only do our sustainability activities focus on the pressing environmental requirements that we are facing and reducing the impact we have on the environment, our team, also, focuses on developing its capabilities and knowledge to extend the life of existing assets in all areas of our work.

Preservation of Our Planet

At RABOTEC, we recognize the urgency of climate change, and, as a leader in the construction industry in West Africa, we champion the role we have to play in decarbonizing the economy for a greener, more sustainable future. Our focus is on minimizing the carbon emissions within our own operations and reducing the whole-life carbon of the buildings, infrastructure and services we provide.

When we support mining companies, build a new railway station or constructs gas pipelines for major infrastructure developments, we seek to do so with the lightest footprint possible. For us, it is about getting a green stamp of approval as it is simply the right thing to do. We are innovating to mobilize more environmentally-conscious practices to preserve our planet and mitigate the urgency of climate change.

Social Commitment

At RABOTEC, our goal is to make a difference for as many people as possible, hence, giving back to the community is always our number one priority. We aim to build social values by trying to bring the world closer together, stimulating action across our business, and inspiring our people. As a company that works in several countries, we have a responsibility and opportunity to leave a positive impact on others and the environment through everything we do. Over TWENTY years as a CEO of RABOTEC GROUP

At the helm of Rabotec Group, a Ghanaian powerhouse spanning mining, engineering, and construction, stands Ibrahim Ali, a visionary leader with over 20 years of experience carving success in Africa. Ali has propelled Rabotec from its humble beginnings in 2002 to become a major player in the continent's economic landscape

His efforts have not gone unnoticed. Ali's leadership has been lauded by the industry, earning him the prestigious CEO of the Year award in 2022. But recognition, for Ali, is merely a stepping stone. He remains focused on Rabotec's ambitious vision: to be the driving force behind Africa's infrastructure development, a trusted partner in unlocking the continent's true potential.
