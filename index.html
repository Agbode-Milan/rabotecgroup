INTRODUCTION TO RABOTEC GROUP

Headquartered in Accra, Ghana, RABOTEC is devoted to lasting success in serving international companies within the industries of Mining, Oil & Gas, and power generation through our expertise in civil work, engineering services, electrical services, mechanical services, structural services, and pipe installation.

We offer our clients access to all of Africa through our affiliates and project-based experience.

RABOTEC Group was founded in 2006 with a handful of employees and big aspirations. Today, with more than 15 years of experience we are continuously striving to upgrade our systems and procedures to provide better services to our clients through the nurturing of a corporate culture pinned on excellence.
